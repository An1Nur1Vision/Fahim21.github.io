About

I am a PhD student at the University of Vaasa, Finland, specializing in computer vision and generative AI. My research interests include machine learning theory, self-supervised learning, generative modeling, explainable AI, neural dynamics, and low-level vision problems. I hold an MS in Computer Engineering from Chosun University, South Korea (2021), and a BSc in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology, Bangladesh (2017). Currently, I am working on self-supervised action classification and text-to-image editing with diffusion models, advised by Dr. Jani Boutellier.

Technical Projects

  • Pose Estimation Dataset Development
    Developed a novel algorithm for view extractions from point clouds, refined with a custom gap-filling algorithm to aid pose estimation, as part of a dataset project at University of Vaasa and Tampere University.
  • Novel Data Augmentation via Back-Propagation
    Designed a custom augmentation network using backpropagation to generate new augmentations on the fly, with KL divergence for distillation to improve model generalization.
  • Investigation of Learning Dynamics for Generalization
    Ongoing project exploring training dynamics, Lipschitz property, loss landscape, and first-order statistical properties to explain generalization in modern deep networks.
  • LiveDet 2021 Fingerprint Liveness Detection
    Achieved 5th place in the LiveDet 2021 competition by developing a solution for detecting fingerprint liveness across various scanner sources.
  • FRVT Face Recognition 2021
    Contributed to the FRVT C++ submission package, refining feature vectors with dimensionality reduction to achieve FNMR ten times better than the baseline.
